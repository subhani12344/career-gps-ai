    Privacy Policy & Data Security

    1. Secure Local Database Encription

    In standalone browser mode, all profile data, resume uploads, and certificate files are stored locally in your browser's private localStorage environment. No remote endpoints collect this data unless you configure an API token.

    2. GDPR & Data Portability

    You have full ownership of your data. Clearing your browser storage immediately purges all tracking histories. You can download your data via the print options inside certification modal modules.

    3. AI Safety & Privacy

    We sanitize personal identifiers before routing queries to Gemini or OpenAI models. Only technical parameters are evaluated.
